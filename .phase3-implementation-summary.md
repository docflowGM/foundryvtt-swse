## Design Decisions

### Why SVG Scanlines + CSS Scanlines?
- **SVG**: Portrait gets integrated holographic effect that travels with the image
- **CSS**: Dialogue scanlines can be easily mood-tinted without modifying SVG assets

### Why Mood Colors Scale Down Opacity?
Holographic glow should be subtle to preserve readability. Using lower opacity values (0.03-0.10) ensures mood tinting enhances rather than overwhelms the base cyan aesthetic.

### Why Idle Pulse Instead of Constant Animation?
A 3-second soft pulse feels more natural than a continuous flicker. It suggests the mentor is "thinking" without being visually noisy.
