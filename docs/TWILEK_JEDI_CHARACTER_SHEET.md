## Notes

**Lightsaber Combat**:
With Weapon Finesse and high DEX, Lyn'ara uses an elegant, acrobatic fighting style. Her Ataru talent allows her to add her DEX modifier to damage, making her both accurate and deadly.

**Force Usage**:
As a Jedi Knight, Lyn'ara has expanded her Force capabilities significantly. Her Use the Force skill (+16) makes her formidable at Force powers, mind tricks, and sensing disturbances in the Force.

**Defensive Capabilities**:
With Deflect, Block, and Redirect Shot, Lyn'ara is extremely difficult to hit with ranged weapons. She can deflect blaster bolts back at her enemies and block adjacent melee attacks.

**Character Concept**:
Lyn'ara is a Twi'lek who grew up on the Outer Rim, learning to survive in harsh conditions before being discovered by the Jedi Order. Her natural agility (enhanced by her Twi'lek heritage) makes her an exceptional duelist, specializing in the acrobatic Ataru form of lightsaber combat.
